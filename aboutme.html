hey there! i'm pradipto, an enthusiast who's turning ai exploration into an art form - with a few laptop battle scars to prove it.

started as a web dev, but machine learning blindsided me faster than a gradient descent. i've managed to burn through a laptop or two in my pursuit of ai enlightenment (cooling systems, meet deep learning), proving that passion sometimes comes with a side of unintentional hardware sacrifice.

thanks to some classic dad-professor nepotism, i snagged my first internship at indian institute of technology (indian school of mines), dhanbad. but i quickly leveled up to cold-emailing ninja status, landing research internships at indian institute of information technology ranchi (another great institute) and a dream internship at bio ai labs, the arctic university of norway. working under prof. dilip prasad - a true titan in this field - was a transformative experience that supercharged my research passion. my first accepted paper? let's just say it was more excitement than most people have on a friday night.

these days, i'm that mad scientist (not there yet, but give me time pls) brewing a cocktail of computer vision, llms, and physics-informed neural networks. building micro-products on twitter (X sounds honestly weird), pushing boundaries, and making ai do tricks that make people go "wait, what?" from awesome research labs to local/personal builds, i'm always exploring the bleeding edge of intelligent automation. i'm not a software engineer. i'm that machine learning guy who'd rather debug a neural network than solve a dsa problem. (seriously, mention some optimized sorting stuff and watch me twitch.)

my motto? innovation over convention, always.
